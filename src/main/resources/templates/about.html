About Us

Welcome to Copenhagen Bars, your premier guide to exploring the vibrant bar scene in Copenhagen! We are a passionate team of students from KEA - Copenhagen School of Design and Technology, on a mission to bring you the best bar experiences our city has to offer.

Who We Are

We are Sarah, Markus, and Agnethe, three friends who share a love for good company, great ambiance, and, of course, exceptional drinks. Our journey began during our studies at KEA, where we bonded over our shared interest in discovering the unique bars dotted around Copenhagen. What started as weekend explorations soon turned into a quest to document and share our findings with locals and visitors alike.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide an insider’s guide to the most remarkable bars in Copenhagen. From cozy pubs with historic charm to trendy spots with innovative cocktails, we're here to ensure you find the perfect place to unwind, celebrate, or simply enjoy a delicious drink.

Why Copenhagen Bars?

Copenhagen Bars is more than just a website; it's a community. We believe in the power of sharing experiences and recommendations to enhance everyone's enjoyment of Copenhagen's diverse nightlife. Our site is built on honest reviews, personal anecdotes, and a deep love for our city’s bar culture.

Join Us on Our Journey

As we continue to explore and uncover the hidden gems of Copenhagen's bar scene, we invite you to join us on this journey. Whether you're a local resident or a traveler seeking to experience the city through the eyes of those who know it best, Copenhagen Bars is your go-to source for the latest and greatest in drink destinations.

Together, let’s raise a glass to the spirit of adventure and the joy of discovering new favorites. Cheers to many memorable nights out in Copenhagen!
